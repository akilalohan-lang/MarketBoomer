The challenge

Don't underestimate the power of procurement

Every purchase you make affects the value and experience you can offer your guests. Procurement is more than simply making an order — it's a complex process made up of many steps. Without control over each one, you risk losing the ability to deliver your best.

Right price, wrong payment system
Not paying the right amount at the right time can sour supplier relationships or result in extra fees — even when pricing is correct.
Good payments, no analytics
When you can't analyse payment trends or identify unnecessary costs, you miss valuable opportunities to save and optimise.
Process without visibility
When errors occur and you can't see who or what caused them, the same mistakes keep happening — preventing continuous improvement.
