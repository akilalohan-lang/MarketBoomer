Real-Time Marketplace

Does your procurement control you?

Supplier contracts, quarterly price reviews and manual quote requests are all normal processes in hotel procurement. But they leave you with little control over your purchasing.

Limited choices
Set cost
One quality standard
No visibility over expenditure decisions
The Solution

Take back control with Real Time Marketplace

Choice
01

Choice

Get a real choice in the products you buy, without the tedium of searching brochures and calling suppliers. You'll never be locked into one price or supplier, guaranteed best price every time.

Live Price
02

Live Market Price

Get live, up-to-date pricing based on real-time data so you can be 100% sure you're getting the most for your money. You wouldn't buy shares based on last week's price.

Guidance
03

Guidance

Marketboomer's Price Premium Alert tells you when you're about to pay more than you should, so you can make an informed decision. If the best priced item isn't chosen, you'll be able to see why.

Why It Works

Key benefits of Real Time Marketplace

DOA / Easy Access

Easy access to best price & quality, and the ability to choose preferred suppliers — simplifying purchasing and removing unnecessary approvals.

Mutual Benefits

Hotels gain control & visibility over their purchases while saving on costs, and suppliers get connected to new markets.

Removed Control

Setting processes around purchasing, budgets and supply preference allows you to trust the process without getting hands-on.

Complete Visibility

Empower your staff to have confidence in their decisions, while maintaining full visibility over every purchase.
