Centralised Data

One platform. All your data. Total clarity.

Stop chasing information across spreadsheets and siloed systems. Marketboomer's centralised data platform gives you real-time visibility across your entire procurement operation.

Talk to sales Explore our solutions
The Challenge

Disconnected data is costing you more than you think

When purchasing data lives in multiple systems — or worse, in emails and spreadsheets — you lose visibility, control and the ability to make confident decisions. Every day without centralised data is a missed opportunity to optimise spend.

No single source of truth across departments and locations

Manual reconciliation wastes hours every week

Reporting is slow, error-prone and rarely trusted

Supplier performance goes unmeasured and unmanaged

The Solution

A single, connected view of all procurement activity

PurchasePlus brings together purchasing, invoicing, supplier data and spend analytics into one unified platform. Every transaction, every supplier interaction and every dollar spent — captured automatically and available in real time.

Whether you manage one property or a multi-site portfolio, centralised data gives your team the intelligence to act faster, negotiate smarter and reduce waste at scale.

Why it matters

What centralised data unlocks for your business

Real-Time Visibility

See exactly what's being purchased, by whom and at what price — across every location — the moment it happens.

Smarter Spend Analysis

Identify savings opportunities, spot maverick spend and benchmark supplier performance with confidence.

Faster Decision-Making

Stop waiting for monthly reports. With live data at your fingertips, your team can act on what's happening right now.

Compliance & Control

Enforce purchasing policies automatically and maintain a complete audit trail for every order and invoice.

Spend Analytics

Know exactly where every dollar goes

PurchasePlus automatically categorises and consolidates spend data from across your entire operation. Drill down by supplier, category, location or time period — and get the full picture in seconds, not days.
