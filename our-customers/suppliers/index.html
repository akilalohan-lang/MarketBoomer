How long do you wait for invoices to be paid?

For many suppliers, delayed payment is a costly but common problem. Hotel invoicing processes can often be clunky, manual and overwrought — leading to compounding problems for you. But your hardships don't end there. Your whole business relies on your ability to offer buyers what they want when they want it — at a competitive price.

Increased risk of late, incorrect or skipped payment

Overhead costs driven up by constant follow-ups

Loss of capital tied up in unpaid products and deliveries

Know what buyers want, sell it, and get paid straight away

Simplifying the process for buyers completely changes the way they interact with you — improving your relationship, communication, and ability to serve and sell. The right software can help you sell more and see those profits returned faster. Marketboomer software was created by a team who has suffered through the problems of buying, selling and invoicing for hotels before.

  • Connect Directly with Buyers

    Online catalogues let your buyers find you and your products through detailed search functions.

  • Decrease Invoicing Errors

    Automated invoicing streamlines your buyers' invoice approval, payment and notifications regardless of format.

  • Control your costs

    View the status of all invoices without following up, and make smart stock decisions based on confirmed pay dates.

  • Incentivise Early Payment

    Take control of your payments by offering automatically-applied discounts to buyers who pay on a weekly cycle.
