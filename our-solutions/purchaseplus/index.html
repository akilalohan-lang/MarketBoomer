The Challenge

Procurement costs time and money

Inefficient software and a high reliance on manual processes means you spend more than you need to on procurement. If you want a good price, you have to spend the time calling and requesting quotes from multiple suppliers. If you want it done quickly, you have to set up a contract and likely suffer financially. It leaves you with a choice: cost or convenience.

Core capability

Save on purchases with PurchasePlus

PurchasePlus gives you the ability to work directly with suppliers to get the product you want at the price you want. Never request a quote or get tied down to a contract again. PurchasePlus guarantees the right product at the right price — every time.

  • Saved Preferences — Mark preferred suppliers so you always get the product you like regardless of who makes the order.
  • Quality Match — Match the standard of your supplies to the standard of your hotel, and easily choose a higher priced item if there's a clear quality difference.
  • Save On Purchases — See an average cost saving of 7–12% on all procurement purchases.
  • Identify Gaps — Keep an eye on expenditure and identify opportunities to save money.

Complete Process Automation

Simplify procurement with a full procure-to-pay workflow, automated for increased ease and decreased errors. Enjoy a total overview of every transaction from start to finish.

Real Time Supplier Pricing

Suppliers should be competing for your business — not the other way around. PurchasePlus allows suppliers to update their pricing in real time, so they can offer the best deal, and you can guarantee you're paying the best price.
